The Movement

G# is the last note of a chromatic scale, but it is also the first note of that same scale as an Ab. So G# Movement stands for the movement of the overturning of time.

Once in a generation the stars align at just the right time through mythical forces still unmastered by man and there will be but a brief moment where an oppurtunity for an event of pure essence of creation can occur; a moment where something that up until that point in time had never existed in thought, reality, or space and it would suddenly exist, metaphorically filling the entire room with its presence. You never know what you are going to create; it could be something good, or it could be something bad. Really bad. But no matter what it is it always is miraculous, for in reality its existence truly is a case of a true miracle. I believe I have traversed one such point of these in time, and what was created at its moment of splender in all miraculouslness is something that is simply too grande not to share and try and give away, for its cost figuratively was nothing more to make, but additives chance, time, and pressure. The creation that I speak of is the album We Shall Prevail by Gadfly & G# Movement. Who are those people? It honestly doesn't matter at this time, but I will tell all. All in due time.

How to Participate in G# Movement

Music is a collaborative process, and none of the material used for "We Shall Prevail" would have been possible without the support and contributions of the many individual members of G# Movement. Every single person in G# Movement was a stranger at the beginning, and because of this philosophy of sharing and spreading music through collaboration Gadfly & G# Movement want to give you the opportunity to get involved with the music creation process. The easiest way to get involved now is to create a BandLab.com account, post some music, and start making connections. Have a writers-block or don't know what to do on BandLab? Then find a post to fork and make an addition to someone elses project. You never know whoes day you'll make by sharing the gift of collaborative music making with another.

If you don't want to join BandLab.com, you know because all it takes is an email address, there are endless other ways for you to participate in G# Movement. All G# Movement songs are open-source, meaning we will not pursue any infrindgement actions if you use them in almost any way to create your own music to express yourself. We want to spread the magic of music as far and wide as we can. Here are some ways you can let music into your life and experience the joy of songwriting and performance.

  • Fork a song and make it your own
  • Share a project you've been working on
  • Sing like no one's listening
  • Write a poem
  • Try and freestyle for as long as you can
  • Play an instrument with someone else

Gadfly & G# Movement stands for everyone and for an all inclusive society where no one feels left out, alone, or uncomfortable in their own skin. Sometimes just talking to someone is the most important thing you can do; sometimes it's playing screamo-deathmetal songs at the top of your lungs untill you can't scream no more. Who is anyone to decide.
