Come Join Us | Who are we

G# is the last note of a chromatic scale, but it is also the first note of the scale as an Ab. So G# Movement is for the movement of the overturning of time.

Everyone can be a member of G# Movement. All you have to do is play along by yourself! Do so by joining BandLab.com and interacting with us as we continue on our musical journey in life, or by creating music in your own environment and sharing it with others.

If you don't want to join BandLab.com, you know because all it takes is an email address, there are endless other ways for you to participate in G# Movement. All Songs are open-source, meaning we will not pursue any infrindgement actions if you use them in almost any way to create your own music to express yourself. We want to spread the magic of music as far and wide as we can. Here are some ways you can let music into your life and experience the joy of songwriting and performance.

  • Fork a song and make it your own
  • Share a project you've been working on
  • Sing like no one's listening
  • Write a poem
  • Try and freestyle for as long as you can
  • Play an instrument with someone else

Gadfly & G# Movement stands for everyone and for an all inclusive society where no one feels left out or alone. Sometimes just talking to someone kindly is the most important thing you can do; sometimes it's playing screamo-deathmetal songs till you can't scream no more. Who are we to decide.
