G# is the last note of a chromatic scale, but it is also the first note of the same scale as an Ab. So G# Movement stands for the movement of the overturning of time.

Once in a generation the stars align at just the right time through mythical forces still unmastered by man and there will be for a moment a brief oppurtunity for a moment of pure essence of creation; a moment where something that up until that point in time had never existed in thought, reality, or space would suddenly exist, and metaphorically be filling up the room, so to speak. You never know what you are going to get; it could be something good, or it could be something bad. But no matter what it is always miraculous, for in reality its existence truly is a miracle. I believe I have confronted one such point in time, and what was created in its moment of all miraculouslness is something that is too good not to give away, for it cost figuratively nothing to make, nothing but chance & time. I speak of the album We Shall Prevail from Gadfly & G# Movement. Who are those people? It honestly doesn't matter, but I will tell all. All in due time.

If you don't want to join BandLab.com, you know because all it takes is an email address, there are endless other ways for you to participate in G# Movement. All Songs are open-source, meaning we will not pursue any infrindgement actions if you use them in almost any way to create your own music to express yourself. We want to spread the magic of music as far and wide as we can. Here are some ways you can let music into your life and experience the joy of songwriting and performance.

  • Fork a song and make it your own
  • Share a project you've been working on
  • Sing like no one's listening
  • Write a poem
  • Try and freestyle for as long as you can
  • Play an instrument with someone else

Gadfly & G# Movement stands for everyone and for an all inclusive society where no one feels left out or alone. Sometimes just talking to someone kindly is the most important thing you can do; sometimes it's playing screamo-deathmetal songs till you can't scream no more. Who are we to decide.
