Life in a Virtual Band

As you may be able to tell some G# Movement members are better connected than others, and with some of musicians only the bear-minimum information is ever known in order to work together. Names are not even required, as is with the case with The Usual Stoopkid, who dropped a hidden gem on the group randomly with their rendition of "From Your Hand To Mine", and was instantly accepted into the group. This has not prevented G# Movement from collaborating, creating, and sharing our musical ideas, dreams, and passions with each other while on this wonderful, and sometimes stressfull, journey to creating what I percieve as a hefty handful of high level musical content. The truly awesome thing about BandLab is that you never know when a random musician is going to be inspired by a project and jump on it to make some mind-bending awesome contributions out of left field that turns a scratch track into a truly unique masterpiece, such as was the case with "Life is a Journey".
