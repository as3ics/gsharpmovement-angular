About Us

We are G# Movement

Gadfly & G# Movement are a group of musicians that came together over the years of 2016-2017 on a musical social media website called BandLab.com. During a very short period of time, specifically in 2017, the group produced enough music at a high enough level that it eventual led to the creation of the album We Shall Prevail, which was originally published in 2019 by Gadfly as an independent album. Since its original publishment the album has gone through a few iterations, with some additions by a few members of the group, to get the work to the final 2022 Remaster album that contains the most up to date work on the project.

G# Movement includes but is not limited to:

  • Thiago Alves
  • Doug Cowan
  • Domenick DeGeorge
  • Zach DeGeorge, aka Gadfly
  • Chase Holton
  • Timmy Jay
  • Samuel P Lauzon
  • Matt Mooney
  • Eli Mwalimu
  • The Usual Stoopkid
  • Tobi Wats

Each of the members of G# Movement brings to the album their own expertise and talents, as well as experiences, and there are many genres of music represented on the album including folk, rock, pop, blues, alternative, metal, and others, mixed and expressed in ways that are sure to provide pleasurable ear-candy to your sound depraved lives.
