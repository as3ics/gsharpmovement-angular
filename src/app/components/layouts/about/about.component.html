Who are Gadfly & G# Movement

Gadfly & G# Movement are a group of talented and independant musicians who came together over a period in 2016 and '17 through a musically themed social media website BandLab.com. During this time numerous creative collaborations, many based on the original compositions of Gadfly, were created, explored, and perfected on BandLab through the various members of the group. G# movement was taking ideas and transforming them into notably ear-grabbing music with the creative additions from the extremely developed and diverse musical talents & abilities of the collective G# Movement. This eventually led to the realization in 2019 that there were enough studio quality tracks for them to produce what we hope is an extraordinary musical album for you today.


During this time in 2017 Gadfly & G# Movement were in the midst of a creative blossom where there were always new musical ideas being shared and collaborations perfected amonst the many members of the group. The creative environment was fueled by Gadfly's hypo-mania combined with the extremely developed & insane musical talents, abilities, and passions of the collective G# Movement membership, which was growing steadily and already had many musical genres & styles represented. This period truly was a magical one filled with many special gifts in the forms of sound & music. Many of the projects from this time made their way onto the final tracks of the inital 2019 release of "We Shall Prevail" by Gadfly & The G# Movement. The albums intention was to reflect the best of the best of everything Gadfly had to offer from his entire musical collection thus far, and is comprised of his most polished recordings in a playlist form that he hopes reflects a story of some of the triumphs over obstacles he has had to go through in life. Tracks making it to the final release include a recording dating from back in 2011 with a new vocal rendition as well as an illusive song from some time spent at Escher House in 2019. The remaining tracks of the album are almost all comprised from the collaborations that originated during the notablably creative period shared amongst the members of G# Movement in the Summer of 2017. Please enjoy.
