Who is Gadfly & G# Movement

Gadfly & G# Movement are a group of talented musicians who came together originally over the years of 2016-2017 through a musically themed social media application called BandLab. Soon there-after, during a relatively brief period of time centered in mid-2017, the group found themselves producing multiple various musical projects in a truly rapid succession, many of which happened to be at a truly high level of musical quality, and where many of the recordings from this time became the origins for the idea of the 2019 album "We Shall Prevail". The album was originally published in late 2019 by Gadfly and was intented to be a project that represented the best of the best of what Gadfly had to musically offer the world, and the album sourced from an inclusive review of every recording Gadfly could get his hands on from every period of his musical career. There included in the album was a track from 2011, originally recorded on an iPhone 3, as well as a completely new original project, while most of the other tracks for the record coming from G# Movement during the high creativity period in 2017.


Since its original publication the album has gone through 2 additional releases that included some welcome, and at times some unwelcome modifications to the album. While one of the additional releases was planned and intended, one of the additional releases was due to the album being pulled from distribution due to the prolonged and often bad-timed mental-health issues of Gadfly. The 2022 Remaster was created to resolve many, if not all, of the quality inconsistensies pushed to distribution during the unplanned emergency re-release of the original album back in 2020.
