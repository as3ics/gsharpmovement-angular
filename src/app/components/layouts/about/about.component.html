Who are Gadfly & G# Movement

Gadfly & G# Movement are a group of talented & independant musicians who came together originally over a period in late 2016, early 2017, through a new-at-the-time musically themed social media application called BandLab. During this time many creative collaborations, many based on the original compositions of Gadfly from throughout time, were started, left, & completed by the various members of G# Movement. Seemingly mundane art was transformed by the tremendous musical talents & abilities of the collective G# Movement group, transforming them into legitimate studio quality tracks that led to the opportunity for Gadfly to believe that he & G# Movement had enough original material to create not only a brand new original album release, but enough strength of material to create an album that had the potential to truly become a cultural smash. During this time Gadfly & G# Movement found themselves in the middle of a creative blossom, where there was always new projects being started by the individuals of G# Movement, and collaborations were consistently being worked on & shared amongst the group. With an environment fueled by Gadfly's ever-present hypo-mania combined with the overflowing collective creative talents, abilities, & desires overflowing from all the remaining members of G# Movement, this was truly a special time that was filled with increased creativity & many new unique musical songs & ideas. The projects from this period additionally were produced at hightened quality levels compared to their previous productions, and with a the collective technical recording knowledge, skills, and methods shared amongst the growing members of G# Movement. Many of the projects worked on during this time eventually led to tracks & projects that ended up making the 2019 inital studio release of the album "We Shall Prevail". This version of this album was intented to represent the best of the best of everything Gadfly possibly had to offer at that given point in time. Additional tracks making it to the final release of "We Shall Prevail" include a recording dating from as far back as 2011, which was originally recorded on a mobile device such as the iPhone 3, and additionally included a completely new original project that arose during my time in Escher House at the beginning of 2019. The remaining album tracks are completely comprised almost selectively from projects that originated during the notable creative collaborative period shared amongst the various membors of G# Movement in the memorable Summer of 2017.
