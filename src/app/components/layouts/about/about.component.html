Who are Gadfly & G# Movement

Gadfly & G# Movement are a group of talented and independant musicians who came together over a period in 2016 & '17 through a new musically themed social media app BandLab. During this time many creative collaborations, mostly based on the original compositions of Gadfly, were created, explored, and posted on BandLab through the various members. G# movement was taking ideas and transforming them into notably ear-grabbing music with the creative additions from the extremely developed and diverse musical talents & abilities of the collective G# Movement. This eventually led to the realization in 2019 that there was enough studio quality tracks for them to produce what we hope is an extraordinary musical album for you today.


During the time in 2017 Gadfly & G# Movement were in the midst of a creative blossom where there was always new musical projects being started and collaborations being perfected from the many idependant musical additions of the group. The environment was fueled by Gadfly's hypo-mania combined with the extremely developed musical talents, abilities, and passions from the collective membership, which was growing very fast at the time. This period truly was a magical time filled with many special gifts in the forms of sound & music. Many of the projects from this time in 2017 wound up on the final tracks of the inital 2019 release of "We Shall Prevail" by Gadfly & The G# Movement. This album was intented to reflect the best of the best of everything Gadfly had to offer from his work thus far, and was a collection of the most polished works from all of Gadfly & G# Movement's collaborations. Tracks also making it to the final release of the album include a recording dating from back in 2011 as well as a project from Escher House in 2019. The remaining tracks are comprised from all the projects that originated during the notablably creative collaborative period shared amongst the various membors of G# Movement in the memorable Summer of 2017.
