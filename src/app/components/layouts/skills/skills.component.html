Where we came from

How we were made

G# Movement members were each drawn to each other simply by the motivational desires inspired simply by unconsious musical desires. Once hooked every member took their own initiative to make contributions, and after months and years of contributions a fraction of a fraction of the countless contributions being repeatedly made led to what eventually made "We Shall Prevail". None of them knew at that time that they were working on a literal album, because none of us were wasting any of this time looking forward; we were experiencing some of the most creative productivity of our lives. You never knew when a new contribution would come in and transform a seemingly normal recording into a priceless studio gem that has a unique & captivating sound. The times truly were magical, and these are some of my greatest moments and experiences from all time. This is what motivates me to share my music.
