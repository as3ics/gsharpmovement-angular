Where we came from

How we were made

G# Movement members were each drawn together by the desires inspired in themselves from their conscious and subconscious musically inspired desires. Once hooked every member used their own initiative to make contributions the music and after years of contributions a fraction of a fraction of the overall countless contributions made led to what eventually became "We Shall Prevail". None of the members at the time knew they were working on a literal album, because none of us were wasting any of this time looking forward; we were experiencing some of the greatest & musically creative productivity of our lives. We never knew when a new contribution would come in and transform a seemingly normal recording into a priceless studio gem with a new unique & captivating sound. The time was truly were magical, and led to many great moments and experiences. Now we want to share the best of these with everyone with our album "We Shall Prevail", now the 2022 Remaster version which includes some new contributions and modification fixes.
