We Are Strong

This is what we're made of.

G# Movement members were each drawn to each other simply by the motivation forces of their ears. Once hooked every member took their own initiative to provide contributions that eventually led to the tracks that made it into "We Shall Prevail". None of them knew that they were working on an actual album at the time, and maybe it was for the better that way. You never knew when an unknown idea from a fellow musician would transform a seemingly mundane scratch track recording into a priceless studio gem. The time was truly magical. The initiative and willingness of my fellow G# Movement'ers to work together on this remarkable project, even if they didn't realize they were working towards this album at the time, has provided me some of my greatest moments and feelings of all my life. I will always truly be grateful to the unique and priceless creativity, talent, and contributions of my fellow band members. Towers will always fall, but this music will always be here with me everywhere I go for the rest of my journies.
