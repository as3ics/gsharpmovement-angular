What We've Done

Songs to put on your Playlist

Here are some of the songs we think you'll enjoy from the very first note.

01. Life is a Journey

Samuel P Lauzon, Thiago Alves, & Gadfly introduce you to G# Movement with this wonderful mandolin piece mixed with a Grateful Dead sounding guitar overlay from Samuel.

02. Crash (And Burn)

Samuel P Lauzon, Thiago Alves, & Gadfly team up again to lay down a new up-tempo rendition of a Dave Matthews classic.

03. Live Love (Live)

This song was only ever performed live once, with Gadfly and an anonymous percussionist, but it was caught on tape. Not bad for only 15 seconds of warm-up.

06. From Your Hand To Mine (Instrumental)

This was one of Gadfly's most inspiring peices, with multiple great versions from talented collaborators across BandLab. This is the original original version, which still can not be duplicated to this day.

09. Every Day's Not Same

It still amazes me that this song blossomed from such an originally short and tepid base track, but this song is a gem and truly stands for everything I stand for. Thank you to all the collaborators.
