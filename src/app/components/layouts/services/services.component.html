What we have

New hit songs, ready for your next new Playlist

Handpicked, here are some of our latest musical creations that we know you'll be bouncing to from the very first beat.

01. Life is a Journey

Samuel P Lauzon, Thiago Alves, & Gadfly come together to introduce the world to the unique sound of G# Movement with this wonderful short piece composition that combines a folky Mandolin with the sound from the Grateful Dead. Songs like these are what G# Movement truly specializing in. A song with a sound truly like no other.

02. Crash (And Burn)

Samuel P Lauzon, Thiago Alves, & Gadfly team up again on this track to present the listeners with a new and original interpretation of the popular, classic, & iconic Dave Matthews Band song "Crash Into Me".

03. Live Love (Live)

Live Love (Live) is a very catchy original improvosational peice performed live by Gadfly and a fellow anonymous G# Movement contributor. This song has only ever been actually performed a single time, but it was luckily captured on a decent iPhone recording that managed to make it to the final album release. Everything in this one works together to bring you into an original and classic jam-band smash. It may have only been performed once, but this song will be enjoyed by all for many years to come.

06. From Your Hand To Mine (Instrumental)

This track has been singularly Gadfly's most inspiring project while collaborating, with contributions from multiple great and talented artists all across BandLab. This is the original original version, which has qualities that I still can not be replicate to this day. An EP of all the different versions of the song inspired by this original track is not only possible, but likely warranted at some point in my career. The song is originally influenced by the Explosions in the Sky song "Your Hand in Mine", which I was inspired to play around with after attending the band live in concert in beautiful Luxembourgh City.

09. Every Day's Not Same

The evolution of this song still amazes me to this day, especially the fact that this song blossomed from an originally rather short and brisk original base track, which was of me on acoustic guitar, but this song is hands down one of my greatest prides as a musician, and with the amazing songwriting contributions of Tobi Wats I can honestly say that this song stands for everything that I personally stand for. Thank you to all the collaborators that worked together to create this gem, especially the gifted keyboard and computer contributions of Domenick DeGeorge & Tobi Wats for creating the foundation of what has matured into an all-time great song.

10. Gonna Shake

Gonna Shake, with the original vocal contributions and songwriting contributions of Doug Cowan could easily be the best song G# Movement has ever been a part of. G# Movement has aquired quite the handful of poppy & catchy tunes, but clearly this song is something special, and stands out among the rest.
