New songs for your next Playlist

Here are some of our hand-picked musical creations we offer to you

01. Life is a Journey (feat. Thiago Alves)

Samuel P Lauzon, Thiago Alves, & Gadfly come together to introduce the world to the truly unique sound of G# Movement with this wonderful short composition that combines a hasty folk Mandolin with a nasty electric guitar for a sound that is truly like no other. If we only had time to share one song that captured the essence of G# Movement this is the song we would share and that's why it's the first song on the album.

02. Crash (And Burn) [feat Samuel P Lauzon]

Samuel P Lauzon ressurected this project from a recording dating back to 2011 that was made on an iPhone 3 to bring us a new and original interpretation of the classic and popular Dave Matthews Band hit-song "Crash Into Me". Because honestly who doesn't love that song?

03. Live Love (Live)

Live Love (Live) is an improvosational piece performed & recorded live for a small audience by Gadfly with an anonymous member of G# Movement. This song has only ever been played a single time, was never practiced before being played, and is captured converstation between Gadfly on guitar and the anonymoud musician on drums. It can be amazing when things come together sometimes, but I am truly grateful that this one was pulled off take one & done to have the ability to be able to get it on the album. You can note that no lyrics have yet come forth for this track so it is still technically unclaimed and open for future development if the opportunity were to arise. Kinda how it works around here, lol.

06. From Your Hand To Mine (Instrumental)

This track has easily been Gadfly's most inspirational idea, with the largest amount of unique collaborations from many great and talented vocalists & artists across BandLab. This is the original original version of the song from the time when the idea of the song was first recorded. This recording has qualities that still can't be replicated by Gadfly to this day. Click Here for link to a playist that has many of the other great collaborations that were generated. Some of these versions were even combined together to even more renditions, and many of the lyrics & vocals were very emotional from the performers. Fun-fact: This song is originally inpsired by the Explosions in the Sky song "Your Hand in Mine", and was made after attending the band live in concert in Luxembourgh City while on a Eurotrip.

09. Every Day's Not Same (feat. Tobi Wats)

What happens when Tobi says he's going to give you an amazingly beautiful and positive original song and then Dom hears it and he's like "Hey! I'm going to take this song Tobi and go G# it to take it to the next level"? We get this , "Every Days Not Same (feat. Tobi Wats)". There are even more contributors and contributions to this song that can't be named at this time, but yea, this song is good, this song is age-appropriate for all, even your youngest, and it is definitely a gift from the G# Movement gods.

10. Gonna Shake (feat. Doug Cowan)

There's only one rule you must follow while listening to this song: You must be in a car, preferrably a convertable, or just roll the windows down, on the highway, driving over the speed-limit, changing between lanes like you just don't care. No, honoestly, another G# classic that could easily be the best they got. And while I don't encourage you to drive over the speed-limit or pass people on the right side (seriously America!) I hope you can atleast appreciate the humor after listening to the song.
