Add to your personal music library

Once you get your own personal free copy of "We Shall Prevail" you can simply add the volume to your very own personal digital music library, where you can keep it and modify it, forever and ever. No give-backs. For real, we should be able to print these digital copies rather easily for the forseable future without anyone trying to return the free something that was giving to them as a super special, once-in-a-lifetime special gift, a gift which should always be given and never returned. The idea's pretty simple, but I made some bullet-points in case you wanted a nice organized list to read.

  • 100% Free w/ no strings Attached
  • No Sign-Up Required
  • No Gimmicks
  • Full Album w/ MIT License
  • Can Simply Add to your personal digital Library
  • Yours to keep forever
  • No Digital Rights Management Software
  • Simply Let the music speak!

Create new music from our music

The creative process is never over with Gadfly & G# Movement. You are and should feel free to take any part of any song that we have distributed in our Free Album Giveaway and do with it what yourself has always truly desired to do with a digital copy of an amazing unknown indie folk-rock album from the literal great abyss; just whatever that may be. I only ask that whatever you do you should take a song that you decide and you should fork it and make it some completely new song that is in no chance completely and utterly your own unique creation and hopefully masterpiece.


And once you do that and are sitting cozy overnight somewhere with a sensational top-100 new hit single in your back pocket I am here to tell you that you have the legal right to distribute that figurative cash cow of hit single however it is that you feel neccessary, hypothetically to produce the most income for your own personal wallet. That's right! Use our shit, to make your shit, and then sell it for real shit. Amazing.
